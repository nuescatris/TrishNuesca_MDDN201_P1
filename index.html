Wellington Networks



CCTV Cameras

Closed-circuit television cameras are used as security devices in almost every public place in the city. These CCTV cameras are the eyes of Wellington as whatever has been recorded in public are sent to larger networks, which makes us all connected and a part of the network.










Social Media

We are surrounded by social media everywhere we go in life, making it a growing network in the city. From objects as small as sugar packets and coffee cups, to bigger signs hung up outside shops, there are links to websites and social media sites such as instagram and twitter.









Manholes

Along with cameras, Manhole Covers can also be found around the city. Since they are situated on the ground, most of us never actually take notice and are unaware of their importance. Beneath these covers are gateways to underground networks of cables, which allow us to send data to and from our homes and connect us to the rest of the world.
