Manhole Covers

Manhole covers are basically the gateways to Wellington's underground world! Beneath these covers are where networks of cables can be found. Their designs usually indicate their owners or what can be accessed through specific manholes.




Wellington City Council

This is a manhole cover belonging to the Wellington City Council.

In 2012, over 22,000 cables were to be installed beneath these manholes owned by the council, which meant needing more manholes to be installed on the streets we walk in. This, in turn is what resulted to the ultra fast broadband that our city now has today.
